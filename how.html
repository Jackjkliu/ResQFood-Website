How It Works

1
Find Your Nearest Food Pantry
Use our website to locate the food pantry closest to you. We've partnered with pantries across the area to make donating easy and accessible.
2
Choose Your Donation Method
Decide how you'd like to donate: either request a pickup from our volunteer team or deliver the food yourself to the pantry.
3A
Request a Pickup
If you choose a pickup, post a donation request on our platform. One of our dedicated volunteer drivers will contact you to arrange a convenient pickup time.
3B
Donate Directly
If you prefer to donate yourself, simply take your food items to the nearest food pantry. Our platform provides all the information you need about drop-off times and locations.
4
Make a Difference
Whether you chose a pickup or donated directly, your contribution makes a real difference in your community. Thank you for your generosity!
Either way, you're a hero!
