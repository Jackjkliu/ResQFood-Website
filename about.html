About ResQFood

ResQFood is on a mission to revolutionize food rescue and reduce food waste. We connect generous donors with local food banks and pantries, making it easier than ever to contribute to food security in our communities.

Founded in 2024, our platform uses innovative technology to streamline the food donation process, ensuring that surplus food reaches those who need it most, quickly and efficiently.
